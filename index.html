Alternative AI Model Providers

  • Hugging Face – Free tier for models like Llama 3, Mistral; easy API key.
  • OpenRouter – Aggregates open models, free to use with sign-up.
  • Together AI – Trial credits for Llama 3, Mistral, and more.
  • Google AI Studio – Free access to Gemini models for small projects.
  • NVIDIA NIM – High-performance LLMs with a free tier.
  • Cloudflare Workers AI – Run models at the edge for free.
  • Cohere – Trial credits for text generation, classification APIs.
  • AI21 Labs – Access to Jurassic-2 with free trial.
  • DeepSeek – Cost-effective, free access to DeepSeek models.
  • Llama API – Simple access to Meta's Llama 3 and others with free tier.
